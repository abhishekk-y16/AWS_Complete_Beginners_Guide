# Books & Courses

- **AWS Certified Solutions Architect Study Guide (SAA-C03)** — solid coverage of core services.
- **AWS Well-Architected** (official whitepaper) — design fundamentals.
- **Cloud Guru / Pluralsight AWS Paths** — hands-on labs for beginners.
- **Coursera: AWS Fundamentals** — intro series for newcomers.
- **Kubernetes on AWS (EKS Workshop)** — for container track.

Tips
- Pair reading with console/lab time; take notes on costs and quotas.
